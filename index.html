The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is the United States government agency responsible for the civilian space program as well as aeronautics and aerospace research.




Project Apollo

Apollo was one of the most expensive American scientific programs ever. It cost more than
$206 billion in present-day US dollars. Finally the Moon landing was made on the Apollo 11
mission and the first person to stand on the Moon was Neil Armstrong.
